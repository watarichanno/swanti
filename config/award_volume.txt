[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]This is the 41[sup]st[/sup] official volume of awards given by [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1042647][color=#ff9900]the Southern World Assembly iNitiative[/color][/url] (SWAN) on behalf of the government of the Coalition of the South Pacific to commend the contributions of nations that have participated in this initiative and helped improve our activity, dynamic, and security in the last month and to encourage these nations to maintain or improve their contributions.[/font][/size]
