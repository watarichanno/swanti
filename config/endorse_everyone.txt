[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]There are too many nations, how do I endorse all of them fast?[/color][/b][/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 1:[/b] Install a browser extension that provides keyboard shortcuts for NationStates such as Breeze++ ([url=https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/breeze++/fmidibfpgbmcmnfedlafdfcbmglbckkf][color=#ff9900]Chrome[/color][/url], [url=https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/breezens/][color=#ff9900]Firefox[/color][/url]).[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 2:[/b] Click on a bunch of nations on the endotarting tool's list or the below list while holding the Ctrl key (or clicking the middle mouse button, or pressing your track-pad with three fingers) to open them in individual tabs.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 3:[/b] Press the [b]E[/b] or [b]C[/b] on your keyboard to endorse the nation in a tab.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 4:[/b] Press [b]Ctrl+Tab[/b] to switch to another tab and repeat step 3.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 5:[/b] Press [b]Ctrl+W[/b] to close a tab.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Step 6:[/b] Open another bunch of nations and repeat.[/size][/font]
[/list]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]With these steps, you can endorse the entire region in max 20 minutes.[/size][/font]
