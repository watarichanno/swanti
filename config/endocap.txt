[center][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=250][color=#1089e6][b]The Endorsement Cap[/b][/color][/size][/font]
[/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]The [b]Endorsement Cap[/b] or [b]Endo Cap[/b] is a limit on how many endorsements your nation should have. Your endorsement count should not be higher than this limit. This acts as a security guard to prevent nations from having too many endorsements and potentially overthrowing the delegate. (Read more about this [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1049890#faq_question_invasion][color=#ff9900]here[/color][/url])[/size][/font]

[center][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#ff0000][b]The current Endorsement Cap is [endo_cap][/b][/color][/size][/font][/center]

[center][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=130][color=#d19d00]To avoid coming too near the cap, you should stop gaining endorsements (but no need to reduce) once it is above the Caution Line[/color][/size][/font]
[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#d19d00][b]The current Endorsement Caution Line is [endo_caution_line][/b][/color][/size][/font][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]What happens if I exceed the cap?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Near this limit, a member nation of the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1192502#crs][color=#ff9900]Council on Regional Security[/color][/url] (CRS) will telegram you about the problem and show how to reduce your endorsement count. Failure to comply may lead to ejection of your nation. You can see below for ways to get rid of enough endorsements for compliance.[/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120][b]Please don't be scared if this happens as it is a common occurrence and is often accidental! You just need to be calm and follow the CRS nation's instructions.[/b][/size][/font]


[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]How can I reduce my endorsement count to comply with the cap again?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]You can reduce it by: [/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Sending a telegram to some nations to ask them to withdraw endorsements from you. This is the best way as you will only lose enough endorsements to comply with the endorsement cap again.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Resigning from the World Assembly (Go to [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=un][color=#ff9900]the World Assembly page[/color][/url] then click [b]Resign[/b]) then join it again. This is the fastest way but you will lose all endorsements.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Withdrawing your endorsement from some nations so that they will withdraw their endorsements from you. This way is non-guarantee as they may not be aware of you doing this.[/size][/font]
[/list]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]Who sets and enforces the endorsement cap?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]The cap is formally known as the [color=#1089e6][b]Hard Endorsement Cap (HEC)[/b][/color] and it equals the endorsement count of the least-endorsed SPCG ([url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1398091][color=#ff9900]South Pacific Coral Guard[/color][/url]) member nation (Currently [endo_cap_ref]). The [color=#1089e6][b]Endorsement Caution Line (ECL)[/b][/color], where you should stop gaining endorsements, equals [endo_caution_line_perc]% the endorsement count of the SPCG nation. Creating and enforcing endorsement regulations is the duty of the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1192502#crs][color=#ff9900]Council on Regional Security[/color][/url].[/size][/font]
