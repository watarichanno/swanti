[list]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The rank of your nation[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The number of nations you are endorsing[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The change of the number above in 24 hours.[/font][/size]

[font=georgia][size=120]Change = Number of nations endorsing in today - Number of nations endorsing in last day[/font][/size]
[font=georgia][size=120]Change percentage = Change in number of nations endorsing / Number of nations endorsing in last day * 100[/font][/size]

[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The percentage of World Assembly nations you are endorsing:[/font][/size]

[font=georgia][size=120]% of WA nations = Number of nations you are endorsing / Number of World Assembly nations in the South Pacific * 100[/font][/size]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The total amount of influence you have helped other nations generate since the first day of this month:[/font][/size]

[font=georgia][size=120]Total generated influence = Previous total generated influence value + (1 + Number of nations you are endorsing) * 2[/font][/size][/list]
[/box]
