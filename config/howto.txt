[center][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=250][color=#1089e6][b]How-to guide on participating SWAN[/b][/color][/size][/font][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]This is a guide on how to do everything this initiative encourages you to. Basically, what you have to do is just endorse and endorse![/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Endorsements are something a nation can give to other nations to show that they like those nations' flag, motto,... It is kinda like the "like" button on Facebook or Twitter, but endorsements have far more important roles than that such as increasing the influence of your nation, the voting power and influence of the Delegate, and making the region safer against external and internal security threats (You can read more about these [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1050443][color=#ff9900]here[/color][/url]), which is why the SWAN initiative's primary purpose is to encourage you to endorse.[/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]In order to endorse a nation, you need to join [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=un][color=#ff9900]the World Assembly[/color][/url]. In this how-to guide, we will show you how to join the World Assembly, how to endorse a nation, how to endorse a lot of nations in a short time, and how to maximize your endorsement gain.[/size][/font]

[box][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]How do I join the World Assembly?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[spoiler=Click to read...]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=130][color=#FF0000][b]Before starting, please note that you can only have one nation participates in the World Assembly at a time! You can have as many nations as you like but you can only have a maximum of one nation in the World Assembly at any given time.[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#1089e6][b]Step 1: Get an Email Account[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]In order to join the World Assembly, you need an email account. You can use any account from any service you prefer. We recommend you to use [url=https://accounts.google.com/signup/v2/webcreateaccount?hl=en-GB&flowName=GlifWebSignIn&flowEntry=SignUp][color=#ff9900]Gmail[/color][/url] since it is reliable for NationStates-related purposes. [/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#1089e6][b]Step 2: Set Email Address for Your Nation[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]To set the email address of your email account for your nation, just click the [b]Settings[/b] button with the gear icon at the top to open the setting page then scroll down, enter your email address in the [b]E-mail[/b] box then click [b]Update[/b].[/size][/font]

[center][img]https://i.imgur.com/hWtjMr7.png[/img][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#1089e6][b]Step 3: Apply for the World Assembly[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]Go to [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=un][color=#ff9900]the World Assembly page[/color][/url] (You can go to this page from anywhere by clicking the [b]World Assembly[/b] button on the left) then click the [b]Apply to Join[/b] button.[/size][/font]

[center][img]https://i.imgur.com/wnQOnbY.png[/img][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=150][color=#1089e6][b]Step 4: Be Admitted to the World Assembly[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]A few seconds after applying, NationStates will send you a confirmation email. Open this email then click the link in there. [/size][/font]

[center][img]https://i.imgur.com/9DBSQWf.png[/img][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]A page will open, click the [b]Confirm: Join World Assembly[/b] button.[/size][/font]

[center][img]https://i.imgur.com/IrbGn1Y.png[/img][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]You're done! Welcome to the World Assembly! If this is the first time you do this, NationStates will give you a banner to celebrate. You now have the power to vote on legislation in the two chambers of the World Assembly: [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=ga][color=#ff9900]the General Assembly[/color][/url] and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=sc][color=#ff9900]the Security Council[/color][/url] as well as endorse nations (Look below to know how to endorse a nation).[/size][/font]
[/spoiler]
[/box]

[box][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]How do I endorse a nation?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[spoiler=Click to read...]
[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]To endorse a nation, just open the page of that nation then scroll down and click the [b]Endorse <nation name>[/b] button![/size][/font]

[center][img]https://i.imgur.com/6jSwPG5.png[/img][/center]

[font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=140][b]Note:[/b][/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]You need to be in [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=un][color=#ff9900]the World Assembly[/color][/url] in order to endorse a nation. Look above for the guide on how to join the World Assembly.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Avenir, Segoe UI, sans-serif][size=120]You cannot endorse a nation that is not in the World Assembly and in the same region as you.[/size][/font]
[/list]

[/spoiler]
[/box]
