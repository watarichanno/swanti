[center][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=250][color=#1089e6][b]The Council on Regional Security (CRS)[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The [b]Council on Regional Security[/b] or [b]CRS[/b] is a group of nations that is responsible for the internal security of [region]The South Pacific[/region]. They are one of the most crucial lines of defense against external and internal security threats. Their main purpose is monitoring, responding to regional security issues, and protecting the Delegate from a coup.[/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]For the safety and security of our region, we ask that all World Assembly (WA) member nations in the South Pacific endorse the members of the CRS, as well as the Delegate. There is however no requirement to do so. But doing so will make you become [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1045928][color=#ff9900]a SWAN Knight[/color][/url] and have some benefits others don't have so there is no reason not to do this![/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]What are the duties and powers of the CRS?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Member nations of the CRS are granted [b]Border Control[/b] power in-game, which means they can eject or ban nations. According to [url=http://tspforums.xyz/thread-4111.html][color=#ff9900]the Charter of the Coalition of the South Pacific[/color][/url], some of the duties and powers of the CRS are:[/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Preventing the Delegacy from being taken by anyone other than the legally elected Delegate.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Reclaiming the Delegacy if the above occurs or the elected Delegate goes rogue and attempts to coup the region.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Establishing an appropriate [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1048805][color=#ff9900]endorsement cap and enforcing it[/color][/url].[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Assuming martial command of the region during a coup d'etat, whether committed by outside forces, infiltrators, or duly elected officials, until such a time that a legitimate government is restored.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Performing all necessary measures to defeat a coup d'etat or hostile invasion.[/size][/font]
[/list]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]What are the requirements of becoming a member of the CRS?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]In order to become a member of the CRS, one has to:[/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Have World Assembly membership in the South Pacific.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Have the Soft Power Disbursement Score of at least 130,000 or an Influence Ranking of at least Apprentice.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Have at least 400 endorsements or half the endorsements of the Delegate.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Have six consecutive months of [url=http://tspforums.xyz/thread-5837.html][color=#ff9900]legislator status[/color][/url].[/size][/font]
[/list]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The specific influence score, ranking, and endorsement numbers may be updated by the CRS, with majority approval by [url=http://tspforums.xyz/forum-27.html][color=#ff9900]the Assembly[/color][/url] (Our legislature), to reflect changes to the disbursement of influence in the region or updates to the game.[/font][/size]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]Sounds cool, how do I join the CRS?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[list]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120][b]Step 1:[/b] Make sure your nation meets the above requirements. [url=http://tspforums.xyz/thread-5837.html][color=#ff9900]Become a legislator[/color][/url] for six consecutive months. [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1077661][color=#ff9900]Actively endorse everyone[/color][/url] to get the necessary endorsements and influence)[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120][b]Step 2:[/b] Submit an application to the CRS that shows you meet the requirements and the reasons on why you should be a CRS member via [url=http://tspforums.xyz][color=#ff9900]our forum[/color][/url].[/size][/font]
[/list]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The CRS will review applicants and choose whether or not an application will be submitted to the Assembly for approval by legislators. The Assembly will vote on applications forwarded by the CRS, your membership will be approved by a simple majority of aye votes.[/size][/font]


[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=170][color=#1089e6][b]How does the CRS perform its duties?[/b][/color][/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]When detecting a nation that is causing trouble or is a potential security threat, a CRS member nations will take action by warning that nation via telegram, and ejecting or banning the nation from the region if they do not respond and curb their actions. In order to do this, something called [b]influence[/b] needs to be used. CRS nations need a lot of influence to properly carry out their duty. In order to have a lot of influence, they need to have lots of endorsements, which is why CRS member nations' endorsement count is usually only slightly lower than that of the Delegate. [/size][/font]

[font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]The threats or troubles the CRS usually deals with are:[/size][/font]
[list]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Nations that continue to exceed the endorsement cap despite being told multiple times not to.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]A Delegate, Delegate-elect, or CRS member nation that goes rogue and threatens to coup the region.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]An external invasion force wants to raid and occupy the region.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Spammers and trollers.[/size][/font]
[*][font=Open Sans, Frutiger, Frutiger Linotype, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial][size=120]Nations that violate the laws of our region and have a ban sentence from [url=http://tspforums.xyz/forum-50.html][color=#ff9900]the High Court[/color][/url].[/size][/font]
